CHBE 453: Capstone Project

A DISRUPTIVE INITIATIVE: BIOBASED PRODUCTION OF ADIPIC ACID
SEPTEMBER 2018 - APRIL 2019


Abstract
Bio-based production of adipic acid is scaled-up to 10 fold of the current global market. A precursor to Nylon 6,6, adipic acid is produced by an E. Coli strain capable of synthesis at the highest recorded titers to date. Waste streams are converted to value added products, completing the circular economy.
